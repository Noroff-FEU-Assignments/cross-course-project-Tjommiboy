[When creating a website there are web security precautions you should take to protect your business and your clients. Malicious attacks can come from both the server side , client side or any other infrastructure and therefore keeping your site up to date is crucial.These are a few steps to achieve this. Sanitizing the data ensuring that its in the right format. Creating secure authorization and authentication. This can be done manually (creating a strong password, dont use your date of birth f.ex), or best achieved through an open standard authorization protocol. Implementing automatic user timeout is smart so if a user who accidentally left their credentials out to see are secured. These are all measures we can take to prevent security breaches. ]
